Sisig Recipe

Home

Sisig refers to a finely chopped pork dish of Filipino origin.

This recipe uses pork belly and maskara (pig face).

It is popular in the Philippines and is now gaining popularity worldwide.

It can be consumed as an appetizer and a main dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ lbs. Pork belly
  • 3 tablespoons Knorr Liquid Seasoning
  • 1 lb. pig face maskara
  • 4 ounces chicken liver
  • 2 pieces onion
  • ½ cup chicharon crumbled
  • 3 tablespoons Ladys Choice Mayonnaise
  • 2 teaspoons onion powder
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • ½ teaspoon chili flakes optional
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 3 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 2 quarts water
